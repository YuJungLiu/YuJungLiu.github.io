Wando

Personal Contribution | Game Direct, UIUX, Sound Design, Animation, Level Design

Team | Yu-Jung Liu, Ya-Yun Pai, Cheng-Shun Ho

“Wando” is a therapeutic and artistic mobile casual puzzle game. The game is themed around nature and ancient civilizations, and players can embark on an adventure with the protagonist "kgoro" to experience various landscapes and puzzle-solving gameplay.

Using spells to transform plants and creating a path connecting the beginning and the endpoint. Through these actions, players can help Kgoro overcome challenges and continue his journey into the unknown.
