Moment

Production Type | Personal Project

In Moment, the night sky ignites with fireworks—bursts of fleeting beauty that dance beyond reach. No matter how you time the shot, their brilliance slips through, uncaptured, a reminder that some wonders defy the lens. So why chase what can not be held? Set the camera aside, let your eyes drink in the spectacle, and etch each vivid instant into the quiet gallery of your mind, where the truest memories glow.
