#Game   #NTU Course Project   #2019   

Treasure Hunter

Personal Contribution | UI Develop, Sound Design, Game Design, Game Art

Team | Yu-Jung Liu, Yi-Jie Huang, Li-Yu Chen

Treasure Hunter is a two-player turn-based competitive game where the goal is to explore hidden areas, and be the first to find the treasure to win. Every round you have 15 seconds to spend energy points exploring the map freely. The map features hexagonal paths with various routes, obstacles, and elevation changes, but no dead ends
